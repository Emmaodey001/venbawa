About

In Akwanga North, Hon. Ven-Bawa aim to be a beacon of progress as a Legislator, focusing on value addition to uplift residents' lives through policies that empower local industries and entrepreneurs across agriculture, manufacturing, and services. These initiatives incentivize farmers, bolster small-scale industries, and promote skills development, fostering innovation and economic resilience. I will also champion social value addition by improving healthcare, education, and infrastructure, thereby laying a strong foundation for sustainable development and prosperity in my community's future.

Hon. Larry Venbawa's Visit to the MD/CEO of the Rural Electrification Agency

Hon. Larry Venbawa, a distinguished member of the Nasarawa State House of Assembly representing Akwanga North Constituency, recently paid a visit to the Managing Director and CEO of the Rural Electrification Agency (REA). The visit was part of Hon. Venbawa's unwavering commitment to bringing sustainable electricity to every corner of his constituency. During the meeting, Hon. Venbawa emphasized the critical need for reliable power supply in Akwanga North, highlighting how electrification would significantly enhance the quality of life for residents. He pointed out the potential for improved economic activities, better educational facilities, and overall community development that could be realized with consistent and widespread electricity access. The MD/CEO of the REA, impressed by Hon. Venbawa’s dedication and the compelling case he presented, assured him of the Agency's support. Discussions were held on potential projects and timelines, with a mutual agreement to prioritize areas that have long been underserved. Hon. Venbawa’s proactive approach and his collaboration with the REA signify a promising step towards illuminating Akwanga North Constituency. The community eagerly anticipates the positive changes that will come with enhanced electrification efforts. Read More

15, Feb 2024

Hon. Larry Venbawa's Visit to the Federal Civil Service Commission

Hon. Larry Venbawa, a dedicated member of the Nasarawa State House of Assembly representing Akwanga North Constituency, recently visited the Federal Civil Service Commission (FCSC) in a proactive effort to secure gainful employment opportunities for his constituents. During his visit, Hon. Venbawa met with key officials of the FCSC to discuss the pressing issue of unemployment in Akwanga North. He passionately advocated for the inclusion of more qualified individuals from his constituency in federal employment schemes, emphasizing the positive impact this would have on their lives and the community at large. Hon. Venbawa highlighted the wealth of talent and potential among the youth of Akwanga North, and how providing them with federal employment opportunities would not only reduce unemployment but also foster economic growth and stability in the region. The FCSC officials commended Hon. Venbawa's commitment to his people and assured him of their support in facilitating employment opportunities. Plans were discussed to organize recruitment drives and workshops to equip the constituents with necessary skills and knowledge for federal civil service positions. Hon. Venbawa’s visit to the Federal Civil Service Commission signifies a significant stride towards alleviating unemployment in Akwanga North. His relentless advocacy and strategic engagement with the FCSC demonstrate his unwavering dedication to improving the livelihoods of his constituents, promising a brighter and more prosperous future for the community. 15, Feb 2024

Hon. Larry Venbawa's Visit to the Chief of Defence Staff

Hon. Larry Venbawa, a committed member of the Nasarawa State House of Assembly representing Akwanga North Constituency, recently visited the Chief of Defence Staff to address the dual concerns of enhancing security and securing employment opportunities in the armed forces for his constituents. During his visit, Hon. Venbawa engaged in comprehensive discussions with the Chief of Defence Staff, focusing on the pressing need to bolster security measures in Akwanga North. He highlighted the challenges faced by his constituency, stressing the importance of a robust security presence to safeguard lives and property. In addition to security concerns, Hon. Venbawa advocated for the recruitment of eligible youths from Akwanga North into the armed forces. He emphasized that such opportunities would not only provide gainful employment but also empower the youth to contribute positively to national security efforts. The Chief of Defence Staff commended Hon. Venbawa's initiative and assured him of the military's support in both areas. Plans were discussed to enhance security operations in the region and to organize recruitment drives, ensuring that the talented and capable youth of Akwanga North have a fair chance to serve their country. Hon. Venbawa’s visit to the Chief of Defence Staff underscores his dedication to the well-being and development of his constituency. His strategic efforts to improve security and create employment opportunities in the armed forces reflect his unwavering commitment to fostering a safer and more prosperous Akwanga North. Read More
