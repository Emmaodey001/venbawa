Hon. Larry Venbawa's Visit to the MD/CEO of the Rural Electrification Agency

Hon. Larry Venbawa, a distinguished member of the Nasarawa State House of Assembly representing Akwanga North Constituency, recently paid a visit to the Managing Director and CEO of the Rural Electrification Agency (REA). The visit was part of Hon. Venbawa's unwavering commitment to bringing sustainable electricity to every corner of his constituency. During the meeting, Hon. Venbawa emphasized the critical need for reliable power supply in Akwanga North, highlighting how electrification would significantly enhance the quality of life for residents. He pointed out the potential for improved economic activities, better educational facilities, and overall community development that could be realized with consistent and widespread electricity access. The MD/CEO of the REA, impressed by Hon. Venbawa’s dedication and the compelling case he presented, assured him of the Agency's support. Discussions were held on potential projects and timelines, with a mutual agreement to prioritize areas that have long been underserved. Hon. Venbawa’s proactive approach and his collaboration with the REA signify a promising step towards illuminating Akwanga North Constituency. The community eagerly anticipates the positive changes that will come with enhanced electrification efforts.

Hon Larry VenBawa disburses N6m scholarship to 461 students of his constituency

Member representing Akwanga North constituency at the Nasarawa State House of Assembly, Hon Larry Ven Bawa, has disbursed N6 million scholarship to 461 students of his constituency studying in tertiary institutions. Hon Ven Bawa made this known during his maiden Annual Value Addition Scholarship Empowerment Scheme held in Akwanga LGA today Friday. Hon. Ven Bawa, who is the Chief Whip of the House said that the gesture was to boost education in his constituency and the State at large. According to him, during my campaign, I have promised to add value to the people of my constituency and what I have been doing is the value addition. I came to add value to your lives. ” You are aware of what I have done and still doing in the area of agriculture, employment, water supply, empowerment among others ” Today, I have decided to disburse N15,000 each to 267 degree students and !N10,000 each to 194 non degree students such as NCE, Diploma among others as part of my commitment to improve on the standard of education in my constituency and the State at large. ” This is also to reduce the burden of your school fees on your parents and guardians. ” Furthermore, it is part of my efforts in complementing Gov. Abdullahi Sule’s efforts in the education sector,” he said. According to him, the beneficiaries cut across Ancho B, Ningha’ah, Agyaga, Nunku, Ningo/Bohar and Andaha Wards. Ven- Bawa charged the beneficiaries to make judicious use of the money to actualize their academic goals.

Hon Larry Venbawa Rehabilitates 60 Boreholes

Member representing Akwanga North constituency constituency at the Nasarawa State House of Assembly, Mr Larry Ven-Bawa, says he has rehabilitated 60 boreholes in his constituency in the last six months in office. This, he said, was to improve on the standard of living of the people of his constituency and others. He said that he who provides water provides life as human being cannot sustain their lives without water. “I have rehabilitated 60 boreholes across communities of my constituency and I spent N6 million to put that boreholes in good shape and to make it functioning and serve my people well. “He who provides water, provides life as human being cannot sustain their lives without water,” he said Ven-Bawa also disclosed that he has initiated different projects cut across education, agriculture, and empowerment among others for the overall development of his constituency and the state at large. Ven-Bawa solicited for the support of all and sundry to enable him succeed. The lawmaker called on the people of the state to support Gov. Abdullahi Sule’s administration to succeed. “This is to enable all of us enjoy more dividends of democracy.”

Hon Venbawa distributes 1,200 bags of fertiliser, cash to constituents

Hon Larry VenBawa, a member representing the Akwanga North constituency at the Nasarawa State House of Assembly, has distributed 1,200 bags of NPK fertiliser to boost the agricultural yield of the people of his constituency, According to him, in this rainy season, it is time that farmers go to the farm to plant crops and dress them. The lawmaker assured of his continued commitment to bring development to the door step of the people of his constituency.

Hon. Larry Venbawa's Generous Distribution for Christmas and Sallah Celebrations

Hon. Larry Venbawa, a dedicated member of the Nasarawa State House of Assembly representing Akwanga North Constituency, recently demonstrated his commitment to the well-being of his constituents through a generous distribution of cows and bags of rice in celebration of Christmas and Sallah. Understanding the importance of these festive seasons, Hon. Venbawa made it a priority to ensure that every household in his constituency could partake in the celebrations with joy and abundance. The distribution event saw hundreds of families receiving cows and bags of rice, bringing smiles and relief to many who may have otherwise struggled to afford festive meals. In his remarks, Hon. Venbawa emphasized the spirit of unity and giving that these celebrations embody. He highlighted his desire to foster a sense of community and support among his constituents, ensuring that everyone could share in the festive cheer regardless of their economic circumstances. The recipients expressed profound gratitude for Hon. Venbawa’s thoughtfulness and generosity. Many noted that his actions not only provided much-needed sustenance but also reinforced the sense of community and togetherness in Akwanga North. This act of kindness from Hon. Venbawa underscores his deep connection to his constituents and his commitment to their welfare. By sharing cows and bags of rice for Christmas and Sallah, he has significantly contributed to the happiness and well-being of his community, making the festive seasons brighter and more joyous for all.
